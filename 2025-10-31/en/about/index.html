About me

I'm at @pocketbroto but Broto is just fine! It is pronounced bro·to |ô| and it means sprout in portuguese! 🌱

I'm an older zoomer and I use any pronouns but he/she are preferred!

I was dropped, raised and also currently live in São Paulo, Brazil! We are the largest portuguese-speaking city in the world and also the city with the most japanese, italian and lebanese outside of their country!
I have a love-hate relationship with this city I won't lie lol
I will mention that I'm brazilian every 5 minutes. I'm sorry in advance, but I wouldn't be a real brazilian if I didn't do it. Did I already tell you I'm brazilian?
Appreciator of local cuisine, football enjoyer and internet troll by nature, I would love to interest you in this mashup that the brazilian culture is :D

Despite being brazilian and my mother-tongue being le brazilian portuguese you'll notice that most of my internet presence is in english! I'm currently working on integrating more brazilian into both my streams on this website.
My english is also 100% self taught! Despite having basic english at school I feel like I never really learned anything there and video games were a BIG part of me learning the language. I remember trying to search up how to pass the damn skyward sword puzzles and bosses, not only having to translate the texts but also having to translate the guides because there wasn't anything in portuguese.
A little bit after I also engaged in reading fanfic and now I'm basically fluent in english, do with that information what you wish.

I have a vocational degree in visual communication! Studied a bit of art, photography, graphic design, art history, marketing yada yada.
Currently I work fulltime as a video editor (for vtubers) but my dream is to work as an editor for the film industry!
On my free time I do vtubing (mostly coworking streams for now but I wanna go back to gaming too) and I *dare* to call myself an artist too hehe
